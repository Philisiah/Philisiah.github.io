Origin

I come from a beautiful Kenyan Village called Kirema in Taita.

I am an Entrepreneuer in training at Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of Technology in Accra, Ghana.

Motivation

I am is passionate about inspiring girls to venture into STEM careers which has seen me participate as a mentor in intiatives like TechWomen's Technovation Challenge and SoteHub's High School ICT program.

I am a member of Sote Tech Queens, an all female organisation that seeks to mentor girls and young women from the village to adopt innovation as a tool to overcome poverty
